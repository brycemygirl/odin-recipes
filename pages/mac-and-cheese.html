Mac & Cheese

My homemade mac & cheese, inspired by a recipe from America's Test Kitchen.

Homestyle stovetop mac & cheese is a disc you might think you have to live without on a vegan diet - the word "chese" is in the name, after all...

Our winning formula was a mixture of cashews, cauliflower, and almond milk that we simmered together and blended until smooth.

Ingredients:

  • coconut oil
  • nutritional yeast
  • dry mustard
  • tamato paste
  • garlic cloves

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Working in two batches, process cauliflower.
  3. Bring four quarts of water to a boil.
